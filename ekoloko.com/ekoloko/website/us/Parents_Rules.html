Ekoloko's Rules of Conduct

Please go over the rules of conduct with your children, and let them know how important it is not to share any kind of personal information while on ekoloko or anywhere on the web. Please also be aware that anyone found breaking the rules will be punished. We are very strict about making sure everyone feels safe and comfortable while using our site.

  1. Always respect other Ekos! Don't be a bully and don't tell anyone what to believe.
  2. Never share any personal information! This includes your user name. Don't use your real name or place you live.
  3. Never use rude or offensive language or discuss inappropriate topics.
  4. Passwords are private! Don't share your password, or ask someone else for theirs!
  5. Don't look for shortcuts. Cheating, hacking or stealing is not tolerated!
