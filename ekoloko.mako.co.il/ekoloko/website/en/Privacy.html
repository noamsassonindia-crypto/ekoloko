Below is a description of the policies of Virtual Tweens Ltd. (“we, our or us”) regarding the collection, use and disclosure of personal information we receive when you log-in into ekoloko.com (the “Site”) and when you use any part of our service or software via our Site.

This Privacy Policy may be amended or updated from time to time. If we make any material changes to this Privacy Policy, we will post a notice of these changes on the Site and in this Privacy Policy. You are advised to consult this Privacy Policy regularly for any changes.

1. Information Collection

This Privacy Policy only applies to information you provide to us online while visiting or using our Site. Information you may provide in visiting or using our Site falls into two broad categories: personally identifiable information, and aggregate information. "Personally Identifiable Information" is information that is personal to you and which can be used to identify or contact you, such as your name and email address. "Aggregate Information" is information that does not identify you, and may include, for example, statistical information concerning the web pages on our Site that users most frequent, and users' activities on the Site. Our Privacy Policy governs both categories of information, as detailed below.

1.1 Personally Identifiable Information

To become a user of the Site we ask you for a user name, password, your gender, your country of residence (NOT the city or street address) and your email address. You are not required by law to provide such information, but must provide it if you wish to become a user of the Site and additional communications as detailed in Section 2.

We also request your birth day, month and year, and your parent's valid email address to notify your parent that you are registered at the Site.

We collect, store and may review records of the communications on the Site that you are involved in.

When a user becomes a paying user of the Site, we engage third parties to collect Personally Identifiable Information from the payer on behalf of such user. The types of information that may be collected and stored at that time include the payer's full name, email address, credit card billing information including mailing address, and such other information as may be requested from time to time. We encourage you to read the privacy policies and other terms of such third parties' websites before using their services.

In addition, you may be offered through the Site to participate in third party surveys and questionnaires which may include disclosure of your personal information. Participation in surveys and questionnaires require your parental consent.

The Site gives you the ability to chat and interact with other users. To stay safe, do not disclose your personally identifiable information (such as your real name, address, e-mail or phone) while on our Site to other users, or ask other users for their personal information on the Site.

1.2 Aggregate Information

Information concerning our users' activities at the Site (such as statistical information about which pages they visit, date and time of the visit, their other activities and how long they view these pages) and information that your browser sends whenever you visit a website (such as your computer’s Internet Protocol address, browser type, the web page you were visiting before you came to the Site) are Aggregate Information.

When you use a widget or any other tool offered by us, whether on the Site or on a third party website, including a website owned or operated by you, we may record data related to that activity, the deployment of the widget or tool and any other account related data.

1.3 Cookies

We allow you to enable "cookies". "Cookies" are small text files that a website can use to recognize repeat users, to facilitate the user's ongoing access to and use of the website, and to compile aggregate data to improve the website. Cookies work as follows: we will send a cookie to your browser that uniquely associates your computer with information stored at the Site. Your browser places the cookie in its cookies file. When you next access the Site on your computer, the Site recognizes the cookie, makes the stored information available (such as user names, password and language selection), and may add new information based on the present visit.

1.4 Communications

When you send email or other communications to us, we may retain those communications in order to process your inquiries, respond to your requests and improve our services (whether or not they contain Personally Identifiable Information).

1.5 Other Websites

We offer and provide access to the Site in connection with other websites. Personally Identifiable Information that you provide to those sites may be sent to us in order to enable use of the Site. We process such information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. The other websites have different privacy practices and we encourage you to read their privacy policies.

This Privacy Policy applies only to the Site. We do not exercise control over links from within the Site. We may present links in a format that enables us to keep track of whether these links have been followed. In addition, third party companies and individuals which we use to perform services related to administration of the Site (including without limitation bill and credit card payment processing), and other websites, may place their own cookies or other files on your computer, collect data or solicit Personally Identifiable Information directly from you. Other websites follow different rules regarding the collection, use or disclosure of Personally Identifiable Information that you submit. We encourage you to read the privacy policies and other terms of the other websites before using their services.

We use cookies to save your user name, password and language selection for future logins to the Site, to better understand how you interact with the Site and to monitor web traffic routing on the Site and aggregate usage of the Site.
